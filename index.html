About Us

With a vision to heal beyond painkillers, Aayushcare is a holistic medical centre dedicated to helping migraine sufferers reclaim their lives. Combining the deep-rooted wisdom of classical Homeopathy with modern mind–body therapies, psychotherapy, yoga, and Ayurveda, we focus on identifying and treating the root causes of chronic conditions — especially migraine.
Guided by Dr. Alex Kurian, a second-generation Homeopathy physician with over 20 years of clinical experience, Aayushcare is more than a clinic: it is a place where patients are seen, heard, and supported on every step of their healing journey — whether through personalized online consultations or transformative residential retreat programs.

Meet Dr. Alex Kurian — Your Migraine Doctor

Dr. Alex Kurian is a second-generation Homeopathy physician with over 20 years of clinical experience, known worldwide for his expertise in treating chronic migraine and headaches.
As the son of Dr. P.A. Kurian, a respected Homeopath with over 60 years of practice, Dr. Alex blends deep-rooted tradition with modern, patient-centered care.

Dr. Alex believes every migraine tells a unique story. Instead of only managing symptoms with painkillers, he takes the time to understand each patient’s physical health, emotional well-being, and lifestyle factors — aiming to heal the root causes that keep migraines recurring.

His holistic approach blends:

  • Carefully selected homeopathy medicines for internal healing
  • Personalized guidance on nutrition, sleep, and stress management
  • Mind–body practices like meditation and gentle yoga
  • Psychotherapy sessions to address emotional triggers and subconscious patterns
  • Patient education to support sustainable, long-term relief
  • Residential Retreat Programs headed by Dr Alex Kurian, providing immersive experiences that combine homeopathy, psychotherapy, Ayurveda therapies, yoga, and meditation to help patients reconnect, reset, and heal at every level.
